Powered by Desiccant Technology

Blue Frontierは、従来の冷媒による圧縮サイクルとは異なり、独自の「塩系液体デシカント」を採用。電力が安価な時間帯に除湿剤を再生してエネルギーを「貯蔵」し、ピーク時に最小限の電力で冷房を提供します。 Unlike conventional refrigerant-based compression cycles, Blue Frontier utilizes proprietary saltwater liquid desiccant. It "stores" energy by regenerating the desiccant when electricity is cheap, then delivers cooling during peak hours with minimal power consumption.
